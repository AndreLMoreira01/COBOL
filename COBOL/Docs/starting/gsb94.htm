9.3 Professional Services Organization (PSO)

Acucorp has a full-solution Professional Services Organization (PSO) led by experienced professionals at Acucorp's worldwide offices. The Acucorp PSO provides a wide variety of consulting and training assistance as part of our business solutions. COBOL specialists with expertise in analysis, development, and project management are available to provide any level of support your business requires. We can help you:

  • Add a Graphical User Interface (GUI) to your character-based COBOL programs.
  • Deploy your COBOL applications on the Internet.
  • Migrate your COBOL application to a client/server and distributed processing environment.
  • Partner with your IT department for joint application development.
  • Migrate COBOL applications written for older platforms to an open systems environment.
  • Interface your COBOL application to a Relational Database Management System (RDBMS).
  • And more...

Acucorp and its strategic partners deliver projects in a collaborative fashion. For more information, contact your Acucorp representative.
