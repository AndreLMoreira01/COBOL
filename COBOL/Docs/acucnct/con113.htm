1.6 Document Overview

Before you can use AcuConnect, you must perform three main tasks. You must:

  1. Prepare your application.
  2. Prepare the server.
  3. Prepare the client(s).

For AcuConnect's distributed processing deployment, these topics are covered in Chapters 2, 3, and 4 of this user's guide. For the thin client environment, you can find information about these tasks in Chapters 5, 6, and 7.

AcuConnect system management tasks are covered in Chapter 8. Details about the acurcl command, which lets you control and maintain all AcuConnect functions, appear in Appendix A.
