9.3.16 Finding the Scope of a Variable

You can easily find the effective range of any variable in the active document as follows:

  1. Place the cursor at any position within the variable whose range you want to find.
  2. Choose the Edit/Advanced/Find Scope command.
  3. The effective range of the variable is automatically selected.
