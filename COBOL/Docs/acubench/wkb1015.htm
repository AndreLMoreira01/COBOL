10.6.3 Align Menu Commands

The following Align menu commands assist you with positioning controls on your screen:

Align Control. Centers a selected control horizontally or vertically or aligns the left, right, top, or bottom edges of your selected controls. This command can also align a selected control(s) to the screen form's grid.

Space Evenly. Spaces at least three controls evenly across or down your screen.

Adjacent. Positions two selected controls so that the right boundary of one has the same horizontal location as the left boundary of the other, or the bottom boundary of one control has the same vertical location as the top boundary of the other.

Make Same Size. Lets you size your controls so that they are the same width or height, or both.

Size to Content. Sizes your control so that it is just large enough to accommodate its label or title.

Lock Controls. Toggles the locked or unlocked state of your control in relation to the grid.

Tab Order. Lets you determine the order in which the cursor visits controls as the user tabs through them on the screen.

View Grid. Toggles the display of the grid in the screen form.

Toggle Guide. Toggles the display of the Screen Designer's control positioning guides.
