10.3 The Screen Component Toolbox

The Screen Component Toolbox is the area of the workbench that contains icons for the controls that you place in the screen form when you create your application's screens. The toolbox is divided into two sections. One section contains standard controls, and you view them by clicking on the Standard button. Most standard controls are supported in both graphical and character environments. Icons for controls that are not supported in character systems are not enabled in the toolbox when you work in a character-based screen form. The other section contains ActiveX controls, and you view them by clicking on the ActiveX button. ActiveX controls are available only for graphical environments.

You can display or hide the Screen Component Toolbox by selecting the View/Screen Component Toolbox command or by right-clicking in the toolbox and choosing Hide. You can also move the toolbox from its default position (right side of the workbench) or change it to a floating window by right-clicking in the toolbox and choosing the Allow Docking command.

By default, the controls appear in the toolbox as bitmap push buttons with pop-up hints. By right-clicking in the toolbox and choosing the List View command, you can change this view to a list of the controls with labels.
