2.5 Sample Programs

The AcuBench product materials include a sample workspace called "samplprj". This workspace contains a project that has sample programs, their source code, COPY files, screens, resource files, and other required elements. The workspace, projects, and programs are intended to demonstrate many of AcuBench's and ACUCOBOL-GT's capabilities. The sample workspace is located in the Acucorp installation directory under Acucbl720\AcuGT\sample\samplprj. We recommend that you familiarize yourself with the sample projects, programs, source code, and related files.

Your Acucorp product media also contains several samples of graphical and character-based reports. You can find the samples in the AcuGT/sample/reports directory. Our samples all use the same data files, namely "sales.dat", "sales.vix", "SalesData.fd", and "SalesData.sl", which can be found in the reports/Data and FD folders.

Other helpful sample programs are available for download in the Support area of the Acucorp Web site. Open your Web browser and navigate to www.acucorp.com/support/public/sample_programs/index.php for a list and brief description of these programs.
