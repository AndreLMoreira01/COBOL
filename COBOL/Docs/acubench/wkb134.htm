13.3 The Section Controller

The Section Controller dialog is displayed when you right-click in the Report Composer window and choose the Section Controller command. The Section Controller lets you add or delete report headers and footers, multiple detail sections, and group headers and footers.The Header and Footer section of the dialog lets you add report headers and footers. If defined, a report header appears only once, at the beginning of a report, and a single report footer appears at the end of a report. By default, the Page Header and Page Footer check boxes are selected in this dialog.

The Detail Section box lets you define multiple detail areas for the body of your report. Multiple Detail sections are useful if you want to give different characteristics to different parts of your report. For example, a Detail section could have different font or color characteristics from other Detail sections.

The Group Section box lets you break out groups or categories of information within the body of a report. By defining a group in your report, you set breaks that are recognized by the AcuBench automatic code generation function. Group headers and footers are defined in this area.

For more information about report sections and the properties that control their appearance and behavior, refer to section 15.3, "Report Sections."
