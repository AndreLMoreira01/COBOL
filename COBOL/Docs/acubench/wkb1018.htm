10.7.2 Align Toolbar

The Align toolbar contains many of the commands available in the Align menu. These commands help you to position and size your individual controls:

Center Vertical. Centers your selected controls vertically.

Center Horizontal. Centers your selected controls horizontally.

Make Same Width. Resizes your selected controls to the width of the dominant (last selected) control.

Make Same Height. Resizes your selected controls to the height of the dominant (last selected) control.

Make Same Size. Resizes your selected controls to the height and width of the dominant (last selected) control.

Align Left. Aligns the left edges of your selected controls relative to the dominant (last selected) control.

Align Right. Aligns the right edges of your selected controls relative to the dominant (last selected) control.

Align Top. Aligns the top edges of your selected controls relative to the dominant (last selected) control.

Align Bottom. Aligns the bottom edges of your selected controls relative to the dominant (last selected) control.

Align to Grid. Aligns the selected control(s) to the closest grid point in the Screen Designer.

Space Across. Evenly spaces at least three selected controls horizontally.

Space Down. Evenly spaces at least three selected controls vertically.

Lock Controls. Allows you to lock and unlock the position of a control on a screen.

View Grid. Toggles the display of the grid in the active Screen Designer window.

Toggle Guide. Toggles the display of the Screen Designer's control positioning guides.
