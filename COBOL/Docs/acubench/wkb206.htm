20.5 The Align Toolbar

The Align toolbar commands are described in the following table:

Description Menu/Command Equivalent

Center Vertical. Centers your selected controls vertically.

Align/Align Control/Center Vertical

Center Horizontal. Centers your selected controls horizontally.

Align/Align Control/Center Horizontal

Make Same Width. Resizes your selected controls to the width of the dominant control.

Align/Make Same Size/Width

Make Same Height. Resizes your selected controls to the height of the dominant control.

Align/Make Same Size/Height

Make Same Size. Resizes your selected controls to the height and width of the dominant control.

Align/Make Same Size/Both

Align Left. Aligns the left edges of your selected controls relative to the dominant control.

Align/Align Control/Left

Align Right. Aligns the right edges of your selected controls relative to the dominant control.

Align/Align Control/Right

Align Top. Aligns the top edges of your selected controls relative to the dominant control.

Align/Align Control/Top

Align Bottom. Aligns the bottom edges of your selected controls relative to the dominant control.

Align/Align Control/Bottom

Align to Grid. Aligns the selected control(s) to the closest grid point in the Screen Designer.

Align/Align Control/To Grid

Space Across. Evenly spaces your selected controls (at least three) horizontally.

Align/Space Evenly/Across

Space Down. Evenly spaces your selected controls (at least three) vertically.

Align/Space Evenly/Down

Lock Controls. Allows you to lock and unlock the position of a control on a screen.

Align/Lock Controls

View Grid. Toggles the display of the grid in the active Screen Designer window.

Align/View Grid

Toggle Guide. Toggles the display of the Screen Designer's control positioning guides.

Align/Toggle Guide
