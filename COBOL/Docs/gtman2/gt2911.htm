9.7 COLOR_MODEL Settings 9 and 10

These settings are similar to models "7" and "8", except that the background color will be grey instead of white. This is useful if you want to use 3D_LINES and want to eliminate intensity considerations.

Equivalent settings

COLOR_MODEL "9" is equivalent to COLOR_TRANS "3", INTENSITY_FLAGS "193".

COLOR_MODEL "10" is equivalent to COLOR_TRANS "1", INTENSITY_FLAGS "193".

See Book 4, Appendix H, for details.
